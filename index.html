Meet Your Coach

BLAKE
MCKENZIE

My name is Blake McKenzie — I'm a Brisbane-based online coach, ISSN Sports Nutritionist, and the founder of LGT Fitness. I wasn't always fit. Growing up, I ate takeaway 4–5 times a week and spent my time gaming. By the end of Year 11, I weighed 105kg. During an 8-week school holiday binge, I gained another 25kg — hitting 130kg.

That moment on the scales changed everything. I decided to take control, joined a gym, and over the years transformed my entire life — physically and mentally. I went on to lose 63kg in total and fell in love with helping others do the same.

"Motivation comes and goes. Discipline stays with you forever."

Today, I use my personal experience and professional qualifications to guide individuals through their own transformations. No cookie-cutter plans. No one-size-fits-all. Just real, honest coaching built around you — your goals, your body, your life.

The Process

HOW IT WORKS

Three simple steps from where you are now to where you want to be. No guesswork, no confusion — just structured support.

01

APPLY & CONSULT

Fill out the application form so I can understand your goals, history, and current situation. We'll have an initial consultation to make sure we're the right fit.

02

CUSTOM PLAN

You'll receive a custom training program and nutrition plan (or macros) tailored specifically to you — delivered through the LGT Fitness app with full guidance.

03

COACH & ADAPT

Through regular check-ins, I'll monitor your progress, adjust your program as needed, and keep you accountable every step of the way. Real coaching, real results.
